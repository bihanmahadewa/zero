the books authored by bihan mahadewa.

upali: the biography (get it)
upali wijewardene is one of the greatest, richest, influencial sri lankan business magnates.
he built a chocolate empire over asia in 1960s, worth over $1b+ and pioneered private aviation & media in the island.
he happens to be the only sri lankan magnate i look up to; this book includes his life, his work and his vision.

the definite path to success (get it)
this is the first book i wrote, at the age of 14. it's a guide on understanding the nature that nurtures winning in life.
i believe this is a book for anyone who want to get a glimpse of how my mind operated at that young age, and how i perceived the world.
